Hide In Fear

Hide in fear is a horror stealth game in which you must avoid a creepy monster hunting you. One of the main inspirations for the game mechanically was Alien: Isolation. I wanted to learn how to create a behavioural tree based AI from scratch that wasn't given exact and precise information on the user. In a similar fashion to Alien: Isolation, the AI uses its senses of sight, hearing and touch to detect the user and close in on their position. Though, it is guided towards the rough location of the player to keep the tension and stress high. Once the player has been under pressure for a while the enemy will then leave the level for a short period of time to give the player some rest and allow time for completing objectives.

Another challenge I set while working on the project was that I wanted as much of the game to be created by myself as possible including some sounds, all of the models, animations, textures, and every script. I chose a PSX style for the game as it allowed me to attempt to quickly create many of the assets in an easier art style. Within the image gallery some of the design documents I made have been included to help illustrate the way in which the AI was created.
