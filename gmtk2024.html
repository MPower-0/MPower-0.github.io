Bloom's Climb (GMTK 2024 Game Jam)

Created for the GMTK 2024 Game Jam, Bloom's Climb is focused on using your powers to shrink or grow objects to solve puzzles. As you solve puzzles you climb up the statue to stop the spread of corruption.

My main contributions to the team involved: Programming the UI and menus for the game, programming some other smaller additions, piecing together mechanics and assets from greyboxes into levels, and working on the sounds and music for the game.
